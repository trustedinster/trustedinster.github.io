About the Product

Pingnan County, nestled in the heart of Ningde City, Fujian Province, is celebrated for its stunning landscapes and bountiful local specialties. Foremost among these is Pingnan Red Rice Wine, a traditional fermented beverage that captivates with its distinctive red color and robust flavor profile. This exquisite wine is meticulously crafted from the finest glutinous rice and red koji mold, undergoing a meticulous fermentation and aging process that yields a smooth, aromatic, and lingering taste.

Pingnan Red Rice Wine transcends its status as a beverage; it is a cultural icon that reflects the dedication and ingenuity of the Pingnan people. It plays a pivotal role in various significant events, from festive celebrations to solemn ceremonies, making it an integral part of the local heritage.

Key Features

  • Crafted using traditional brewing techniques, ensuring quality
  • Made from superior glutinous rice and red koji mold
  • Full-bodied with a rich aroma
  • Smooth on the palate with a long finish
  • Ideal for various important events
